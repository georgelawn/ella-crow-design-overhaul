Privacy Policy

Last updated: 15 June 2026

Ella Crow Social Tracker is a private artist-management dashboard used to view performance data for Ella Crow's authorised social media accounts.

Data accessed

When Ella Crow authorises TikTok, the tracker accesses basic profile information, follower and account statistics, and public video information including views, likes, comments and shares.

How data is used

The data is used only to display artist performance trends in the private management dashboard. It is not sold, used for advertising or shared with unrelated third parties.

Storage and security

Authorisation tokens are stored securely in Supabase and are not exposed in the public dashboard. Performance snapshots may be retained for up to 400 days to support month-on-month comparisons.

Deletion

Access can be revoked through TikTok at any time. Data-deletion requests can be made through the official Ella Crow website.
